# Half Hinges of the Bible — Refuting the Half-Truths

## Entry 9

**Anchor:** And you hath he quickened, who were dead in trespasses and sins. (Ephesians 2:1)

**Title:** Half Hinges of the Bible — Refuting the Half-Truths

### The Story

Brother Sonny Pyles once stood before the barn of Scripture and showed us a bucket full of half-hinges — pieces that looked useful but could never hang a door. He took the half-verses men twist to imply ability in dead men or faith before life, and he gave the full context.

He finds dead men — unable, hostile, lifeless. He does everything — quickens whom He will before any response is possible. The man wakes up different — new birth is real, new hunger appears. The baby is real but it is a baby — needs milk, can grieve the Spirit. God will finish what He started — none of His sheep are lost.

The rest of the story? Life precedes response. Salvation is finished in the spotless offering of the Lord Jesus Christ. No half-hinge will ever open the door of grace.
