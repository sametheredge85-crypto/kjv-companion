Bible Questions

Ask any Bible question. This tool will guide you to a whole‑counsel, KJV‑anchored, contextual answer.

How to Use This Page

After typing your question, copy it and ask the Companion:

“Answer this using the whole counsel of the KJV.”

The Companion will:

  • restore the full context of the passage
  • identify speaker, audience, purpose, and setting
  • harmonize related passages across the KJV
  • avoid cherry‑picking or isolated proof‑texts
  • explain any apparent contradictions
  • give a consistent, non‑denominational answer

If the question relates to a specific passage, include the reference. Example:

“Explain John 3:1–21 in full context.”

If the question is doctrinal, ask:

“Show the whole‑Bible harmony on this topic.”
