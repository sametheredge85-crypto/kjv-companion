Preaching Assistant

PRIVATE — For Preacher Use Only

1. First Principles Verification

Ask the Companion:

  • “Apply the First Principles Pattern to this sermon.”
  • “Check for speaker, audience, purpose, setting, and flow.”
  • “Identify any half‑hinges or isolated verses.”

2. Context Restoration

Ask:

“Restore the full context of every passage used in this sermon.”

This ensures every verse is used in harmony with its scene, audience, and purpose.

3. Page Flow Analysis

Ask:

“Show the narrative or argument flow behind each passage, ignoring chapter and verse breaks.”

4. Doctrinal Theme Mapping

Ask:

“Identify the doctrinal themes present in this sermon and how they interlock.”

  • life vs death
  • light vs darkness
  • flesh vs spirit
  • time vs eternity
  • law vs grace
  • discipleship vs sonship

5. Time vs Eternity Check

Ask:

“Separate eternal finished work from timely conditional experience in this sermon.”

6. Mixed Crowd & Audience Check

Ask:

“Identify all audience types referenced in this sermon’s passages.”

Jesus often speaks to disciples, Pharisees, and crowds at the same time.

7. Whole‑Bible Harmony

Ask:

“Show the whole‑Bible harmony for the doctrines in this sermon.”

8. Annex Entry Generator

After verification, ask:

“Generate the Annex entry for this sermon.”

This will give you:

  • the HTML block
  • the filename
  • the summary
  • the tags
  • the harmonized doctrinal notes

Paste the generated file into your private Annex folder and update annex-private.html.

This private tool is for sermon preparation, doctrinal harmony, and pastoral study. Whole counsel. Whole context. Whole harmony. Truth remains true.
