## Macedonia Primitive Baptist Church (Original Doctrine)
**Section, Jackson County, Alabama**  
Constituted in the 1860s • Relocated to current building in 1999

**Quick Info**
- **Worship**: Every Sunday **except 5th Sundays** • 10:30 AM
- **Address**: 3970 CR 38, Section, AL 35771 (Map pin: 3972 CR 38)
- **Call / Text (Pastor Sam Etheredge)**: [(864) 906-4911](tel:+18649064911)

We continue the **historic Old School Primitive Baptist faith** at our current location, emphasizing sovereign grace, particular redemption, predestination, and the doctrines once delivered to the saints. Traditional a cappella singing and expository preaching. Everyone is welcome.

<details>
<summary><strong>Important note on local history</strong> (tap to expand)</summary>

There are two entities in the Section/Macedonia area using similar names. The original historic building and associated cemetery were taken over by a group that adopted Universalist ("no hellers") views, diverging from the original Primitive Baptist doctrine. Our congregation faithfully maintains the **original doctrine** at the relocated building on CR 38.

</details>
