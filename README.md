## Macedonia Primitive Baptist Church (Original Doctrine)
**Section, Jackson County, Alabama**  
Constituted in the 1860s • Relocated to current building in 1999  

We continue the **historic Old School Primitive Baptist faith** at our current location, emphasizing sovereign grace, particular redemption, predestination, and the doctrines once delivered to the saints. Traditional a cappella singing and expository preaching. Everyone is welcome.

**Important note on local history**: There are two entities in the Section/Macedonia area using similar names. The original historic building and associated cemetery were taken over by a group that adopted Universalist ("no hellers") views, diverging from the original Primitive Baptist doctrine. Our congregation faithfully maintains the **original doctrine** at the relocated building on CR 38.

### Service Times & Association
- Worship services: 2nd, 3rd, and 4th Sundays (and some 5th Sundays) at 10:30 AM  
- Part of the Sand Mountain Association of Primitive Baptist Churches  
- Annual associational meeting: Usually the 2nd Sunday weekend in July (check Facebook group for details)
